About Hindia

Hindia is an indie music group from Indonesia that was founded in 2016 by Baskara Putra. The group consists of Baskara as the vocalist and guitarist, along with his friends Pandu Fathoni as the bassist, Reindy Wiranata as the guitarist, and Adhe Arrio as the drummer.Hindia is an Indonesian indie band known for their music which incorporates elements of traditional Indonesian music with indie rock and pop.

Their songs have deep and touching lyrics, and they have gained popularity in Indonesia since releasing their debut album "Menari Dengan Bayangan" in 2018. Some of their popular songs include "Secukupnya," "Evaluasi,". Hindia has received several music awards and nominations for the Indonesian music award, Anugerah Musik Indonesia (AMI).

Hindia Songs

The song "Evaluasi" by Hindia tells the story of someone who reflects on the choices they have made in their life and looks at the impact they have had on their current situation. The song carries a message of learning from mistakes and striving to become better, and has a melancholic musical tone with a slow tempo and the use of acoustic guitar and piano.

The song "Rumah ke Rumah" by Hindia tells the story of someone who is searching for a place to settle down and find the meaning of their life. The song carries a message to keep searching and exploring in life, without being afraid to try new things. Overall, the song encourages listeners to consider the meaning of "home" and how it can be a starting point to find our true purpose in life.

The song "Untuk Apa" by Hindia tells the story of someone who is questioning the meaning of a long-term relationship and wondering if it still holds the same value as before. The song contains a message about the importance of communication and solving problems together in a relationship. Overall, the song encourages listeners to reflect on their relationships.
